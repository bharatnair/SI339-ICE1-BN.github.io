  • On Tintin

The Adventures of Tintin was a series of graphic novels created by Franco-Belgian cartoonist, Hergé. The series is marked by Hergé's lovely ligne claire art style and it's host of endearing characters and wonderous locations. A few of my favorite ones are Destination Moon, Cigars of the Pharoah, Tintin in Tibet, and Tintin and the Black Island

I've adored them ever since my father once showed it to me when I was about six or seven years old. He told me how he, as a child, was so drawn by the adventure, camaraderie, and humor. He loved them, and he simply had to share with his son.

One day, I hope I may have the pleasure to continue that tradition and share it with my own child.

Bharat Nair — SI 339, Winter 2018
